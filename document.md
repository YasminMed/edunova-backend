EduNova Mobile App
Smart, AI-powered educational ecosystem that connects students and teachers in one collaborative, engaging, and personalized learning environment.
EduNova empowers students to take control of their learning journey while helping teachers manage, guide, and inspire through intelligent tools, progress analytics, and creative communication channels.
________________________________________
1. Student Section
Dashboard
•	Personalized dashboard showing today’s schedule, pending assignments, upcoming exams, and motivational quote of the day.
•	AI highlights daily learning goals and sends “Smart Study Tips.”
________________________________________
Marks & Performance
•	General Marks View: Overview of grades per subject.
•	Detailed Marks View: Separate sections for quizzes, midterms, and finals.
•	Rank & Medal System: Gamified ranking based on academic performance and participation (e.g., Gold for top 10%, Silver for top 25%).
•	Progress Graphs: Visual timeline of student improvement.
•	AI Feedback: Weekly report summarizing strong and weak areas.
________________________________________
AI Assistant
•	Provides daily motivational messages and personalized study reminders.
•	Offers 24/7 help for lecture-related questions, powered by context-aware AI.
•	Recommends extra materials, videos, or exercises based on performance trends.
________________________________________
Schedule & Deadlines
•	Displays all upcoming exams, quizzes, and project deadlines.
•	Deadlines of paying fees 
•	Calendar sync with notifications.
•	AI suggests study plans before each major exam.
________________________________________

Posts Feed
•	Stream of updates, announcements, and posts shared by teachers.
•	Allows students to like, comment, and save posts for later.
•	Smart filter for posts by subject or teacher.
________________________________________
Music & Focus Zone
•	Curated playlists: Classical, Nature Sounds, Lo-Fi, Instrumental, White Noise.
•	Option to upload your own music or link from Spotify.
•	Focus Timer: Study timer that plays background music and tracks productivity.
________________________________________
Chat & Communication
Organized into three sections:
1.	Classmates Chat: Direct messages with peers.
2.	Groups Chat: Class or project groups with shared files and announcements.
3.	Teachers Chat: Direct academic communication channel.
•	Optional voice note and file sharing features.
•	Built-in auto-translate for multilingual classes.
________________________________________
Profile & Personal Info
•	Editable profile with academic info, picture, and achievements.
•	Portfolio area for uploaded work, certificates, or projects.
•	Option to link with LinkedIn or university portal.
•	See the fee payed and what left for paying with deadline
________________________________________
Courses
Each course includes:
•	Course Overview: description, syllabus, objectives.
•	Assignments: list with due dates and submission status.
•	Quizzes: interactive or uploaded quizzes.
•	Attendance: visual record of presence and participation.
•	Resources: uploaded lectures, slides, and notes.
________________________________________

Teacher Info & Feedback
•	Quick view of each teacher’s profile (name, office hours, contact).
•	Option to submit anonymous feedback on teaching style, clarity, and engagement.
________________________________________
Additional Enhancements
•	Achievements Board: Shows unlocked medals or milestones.
•	Study Groups Recommendation: AI suggests classmates to study with based on similar weaknesses.
•	Offline Mode: Download lectures and assignments for offline access.
________________________________________
2. Teacher Section
Dashboard
•	Overview of active classes, assignments pending review, and recent student messages.
•	AI analytics panel showing class performance trends and alerts about struggling students.

Content Management
Teachers can:
•	Upload lectures, PDFs, slides, or recorded videos.
•	Create and publish posts, assignments, and quizzes.
•	Schedule content visibility (e.g., “visible after class on Tuesday”).
•	Enable auto-grading for objective quizzes.

Chat & Collaboration
Divided into:
1.	Students Chat: one-on-one help or feedback.
2.	Group Chat: class discussions or project groups.
3.	Teachers Chat: collaboration with colleagues for cross-subject projects.
•	Built-in announcement tool to send instant notifications to all students.
•	AI-assisted reply suggestions for common questions.

Marks & Evaluations
•	Upload and manage marks (quiz, midterm, final).
•	Auto-calculate averages and generate performance charts.
•	AI suggestions for extra support to low-performing students.
________________________________________
Profile Management
•	Editable personal info, specialization, achievements, and research.
•	Option to share publications or articles with students.

AI Insights & Reports
•	Weekly AI reports highlighting:
o	Top performing students.
o	Students at risk or needing help.
o	Attendance analysis.

________________________________________
Assignments & Attendance
